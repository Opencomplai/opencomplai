# License

Opencomplai is open-core: a community edition licensed for openness and auditability, and an enterprise edition for teams who need a commercial licence.

It covers the full risk assessment engine, CLI, SDK, services, and the [EU AI Act Checker](getting-started/eu-ai-act-checker.md).

The AGPL-3.0 is a copyleft licence: if you modify Opencomplai and make it available to users over a network, you must make your modified source available to those users under the same licence. See the [full licence text](https://github.com/Opencomplai/opencomplai/blob/main/LICENSE) for the exact terms.

## Enterprise Edition

For use cases the AGPL doesn't fit — including a hosted premium dashboard, single sign-on, additional rule engines, and commercial support — Opencomplai Enterprise Edition is available under a commercial licence. See [opencomplai.com](https://opencomplai.com) for details.

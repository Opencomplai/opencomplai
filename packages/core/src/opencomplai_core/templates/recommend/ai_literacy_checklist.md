## What EU AI Act {{article}} requires

Providers and deployers must ensure a sufficient level of AI literacy among staff and
other persons operating or using the AI system on their behalf, proportionate to their
role, technical knowledge, and the context of use (Art. 4).

## Suggested implementation

- [ ] Identify every role that operates, configures, or reviews outputs of this system.
- [ ] Provide role-appropriate training covering: what the system does, its known
      limitations, how to recognise and escalate anomalous outputs, and applicable
      compliance obligations.
- [ ] Record completion of this training (who, when) as part of your evidence trail.
- [ ] Revisit the training content whenever the system's `intended_purpose` or risk
      classification changes.
